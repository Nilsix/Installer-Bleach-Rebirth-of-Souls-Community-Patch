Since Tamsoft didn't want to give us a new patch, we made one ourselves with a launcher.

NEW PATCH : 
-bug fixes (everyone can now punish flash steps, whiffed buttons into hakugeki are removed, timestops are removed, grab dash -> sp1/sp2 are removed, byakuya sp1 giving him more reverse was removed, and many more)
-Balance changes (top 4 got nerfed, not to the ground, but to a healthier ground), most characters also got small tweaks
-changes to the evolution values so that the game doesn't promote a lose to win playstyle anymore
-removed guaranteed guardbreak/unblockable setups

here are the complete changes : https://rebalance-of-souls.github.io/reBalanceOfSouls.github.io/

Performance improvement settings (for people with lower end pcs or those who just want to increase their FPS)
